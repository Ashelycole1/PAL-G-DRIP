Pal G Drip House was born on the streets of Kampala with one mission: to give Uganda's youth a fashion brand they can truly call their own. We blend contemporary streetwear aesthetics with Afrocentric pride to create clothing that speaks before you even open your mouth.

From graphic hoodies and custom tees to tailored joggers and exclusive accessories, every piece in our collection is designed with intention, quality, and culture at its core. We don't follow trends — we set them.

Whether you're heading to a concert, rocking the streets, or just staying fresh at campus — Pal G Drip House has your fit covered. Our commitment to quality ensures that every garment is made to last, using 100% premium cotton and locally sourced materials wherever possible.
